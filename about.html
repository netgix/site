About the event

    A two-day National Level Workshop on Advanced IP Networking

    With Hands-on training in Cisco Packet Tracer, IP address calculator, VLAN and more

    Conducted by the Dept. of CSE, SNS COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY

  • Presented and taught by a reputed resource person from Wipro, Bengaluru.
  • The workshop will be conducted for two days - 28/09/2018 and 29/09/2018
  • A entry fee of Rs. 350 per head will be required for registration
  • Lunch and Kits will be provided
  • You are encouraged to bring your laptop. It is recommended, but optional.

Topics to be covered:

  • Basic concepts and components of data communication.
  • TCP/IP protocol suite
  • Practical sessions on network components
  • IP Addressing - IPv4
  • LAN connectivity- demo and practice
  • Study of VLAN - theory with practice
  • HTTP AND FTP- Theory, demo and practice
  • DHCP- Theory , demo and practice
  • PROXY SERVER - theory,demo and practice
  • Firewall and PROLANNET
  • IPV6
  • Information and Cyber security.
